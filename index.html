The Person Behind It

BhuwanRegmi.

LinkedIn GitHub Get in touch

I build products because I naturally think in solutions. Anytime I see something slow, repetitive, or needlessly annoying, my mind jumps to "there should be a platform for this" — and I end up shipping it. Sometimes even after dreaming about it.

GymSage started because I used to send workout photos to a friend every day for feedback — but manually logging felt tedious. I wanted to just speak my sets out loud and have AI handle the rest. So I built it solo, from scratch: voice logging, GPT-4 Vision body analysis, a coaching layer with memory. The whole thing.

GutLens came from a hospital visit. An IBS-D diagnosis, a lot of confusing dietary research, and the exhaustion of figuring out what I could safely eat. I built a weekend tool for myself — point your camera at food, get instant FODMAP safety info. No accounts, no complexity.

I've built a handful of other tools that helped people save time or money — along with the classic collection of projects that ran perfectly on my laptop and immediately collapsed in production. A rite of passage I now take with humor.

What I bring to a team or co-founder partnership is a mix of initiative, ownership, and curiosity. I don't wait for perfect instructions — I explore, test, break things, fix them, and ship. I care about usability, scalability, and whether someone out there genuinely benefits from what I'm building.

"If the challenge is interesting and the impact is real — I'm all in."
