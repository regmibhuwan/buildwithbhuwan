About

I'm a self taught fullstack developer with a focus on machine learning and artificial intelligence. Over the past year, I've completed seven certifications spanning supervised learning, unsupervised learning, reinforcement learning, linear algebra for ML, and generative AI.

What makes my work different is context. I work on the production floor at a poultry processing company. Everything I build comes from real gaps I see firsthand, not classroom exercises.

EVP Connect, a full employee communication platform, started because I watched teams struggle with fragmented scheduling and missed announcements every single day. I built it solo, then presented it to our CFO and Senior Director of People Services. It is now under technical and security review. My vendor data analysis identified $2.71M in actionable savings. My AI apps serve real users today.

I learn fast, I ship faster, and I care deeply about whether the things I build actually help people.

"If the challenge is interesting and the impact is real, I'm all in."
